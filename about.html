About Ultra Klensing

For over 15 years, we've been setting the standard for professional cleaning services, combining industry expertise with unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ultra Klensing began with a simple mission: to provide cleaning services that exceed expectations while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and environmental responsibility.

What started as a small residential cleaning service has grown into a comprehensive cleaning company serving hospitals, corporate offices, and homes throughout the metropolitan area. Our growth has been built on trust, reliability, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we're proud to be the preferred cleaning partner for over 2,500 satisfied clients, including major healthcare facilities, Fortune 500 companies, and countless families who trust us to maintain their most important spaces.
