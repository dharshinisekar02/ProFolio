Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule cleaning services?

The frequency depends on your needs and lifestyle. Most residential clients choose bi-weekly or monthly services, while commercial clients typically require daily or weekly cleaning. We can help you determine the best schedule during your consultation.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. We use professional-grade, eco-friendly products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment. If you have specific product preferences, we're happy to accommodate.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?

We guarantee your satisfaction. If you're not completely happy with our service, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean any areas of concern at no additional charge.

Are your cleaning staff insured and bonded?

Absolutely. All our cleaning professionals are fully insured, bonded, and undergo thorough background checks. We carry comprehensive liability insurance to protect your property and provide peace of mind.

How do I prepare for a cleaning service?

Minimal preparation is needed. We recommend securing valuables, clearing surfaces of personal items, and providing access to all areas to be cleaned. Our team will take care of the rest!

Do you offer discounts for regular services?

Yes! We offer discounts for recurring services: 10% off weekly services, 5% off bi-weekly services. We also provide seasonal promotions and loyalty rewards for long-term clients.
